Quick Install Guide

INSTALL LUNAS TALAAN

Scan to Download

1

Open your phone's camera and scan the QR code above.

2

Tap on the notification that appears to open the download link.

3

Follow the on-screen instructions to install the application.

4

Enable "Install Unknown Apps" in your device settings if prompted.

5

Once installed, open Lunas Talaan and sign up or log in.
